Global pharma greenfield S/4HANA — 14 countries, 22-month programme

GreenfieldPharmaS/4HANA 2023Multi-country

A top-20 pharmaceutical company replaced six ERP systems across 14 countries with a single S/4HANA instance on RISE with SAP. Solasto led the full programme — advisory through hypercare — using our Pharma sector template and a dedicated GxP validation workstream.

Key challenge: harmonising 14 local chart-of-accounts variants while maintaining country-specific legal reporting. Resolved via a universal journal design with document splitting.

Industrial manufacturer brownfield conversion — zero-downtime cutover

BrownfieldManufacturingS/4HANA 2022On-prem
