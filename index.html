Global S/4HANA greenfield for a FTSE 250 manufacturing group

Manufacturing S/4HANA 2023 18 countries 36 months
34%

On time
Delivered to original milestone

A FTSE 250 industrial manufacturer needed to retire four legacy ERP systems across 18 countries and consolidate onto a single S/4HANA instance. The programme spanned finance, supply chain, manufacturing and HR — with a hard regulatory cutover deadline in the UK and Germany.

Solasto led the global template design, data migration strategy, and hypercare operations team. We delivered the 18-country rollout on the original milestone date with zero critical post-go-live incidents and a finance close cycle reduced from 12 days to 8.
